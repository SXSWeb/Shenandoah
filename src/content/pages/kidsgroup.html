Kids Ministry

Kids Ministry in Cedar Park

If you’re looking for a welcoming kids church in Cedar Park, Shenandoah Baptist Church provides a safe and supportive environment where children learn about God, build friendships, and grow in faith… and snacks, don’t forget snacks.

Our children’s classes are small so every child receives personal attention and encouragement.

Sunday Schedule

Sunday School

Led by: Carole Schieffer

9:15 AM – 10:30 AM

Children learn Bible stories and participate in discussion and activities designed for their age.

Kids Church

Led by: Warrior's Christian Academy

11 AM – 12 PM

Children begin the worship service with their families and are dismissed after the music portion to attend Kids Church.

Wednesday Night Programs

Mission Friends (Preschool – 1st Grade)

Led by: Maria Brookes, Rebekah Diaz, Tiffany Cedillo

6:30pM – 7:30PM

Mission Friends introduces younger children to God’s love through stories of missionaries from around the world, music, play, and simple activities. Oh yeah, and snacks. We love to snack.

Children who are potty trained or wearing pull-ups through first grade are welcome.

Children in Action (Elementary Age)

Led by: Kathy Wilcox

6:30pM – 7:30PM

Kathy is a certified special needs teacher and warmly welcomes children of all abilities. If your child may need accommodations, families are encouraged to reach out ahead of time so she can prepare and ensure every child feels comfortable and supported.

Why Families Appreciate Our Kids Ministry

  • Small class sizes
  • Caring and experienced teachers
  • A welcoming environment for children of all abilities
  • Opportunities for kids to build real friendships
