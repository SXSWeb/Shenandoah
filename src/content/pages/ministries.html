Church Ministries

At Shenandoah Baptist Church, our ministries are designed to help people of all ages grow in faith, build friendships, and serve one another.

Whether you're looking for Bible study, programs for your children, or opportunities to connect with other adults, there is a place for you here.

Explore the ministries below to learn more about how you and your family can get involved.

Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of our neighbors and the nations through evangelism, baptism, and biblical teaching.

Sunday Bible Study

Sunday mornings begin with Bible study for all ages. Adults gather for discussion-based classes, while children and youth participate in age-appropriate Sunday school.

A nursery is available for younger children.

Learn more about Sunday morning classes and what to expect.

Sunday Worship

Our Sunday worship service is the heart of our week. We gather to sing together, pray, and hear biblical teaching that encourages and challenges us in our daily lives.

Children begin in the service and are later dismissed to Kids Church during the message.

Learn more about our worship services.

Children's Ministry

Our children's ministry provides a welcoming and supportive environment where kids can learn about God, build friendships, and grow in their faith.

Classes are intentionally small so that every child is known and supported.

Learn more about Kids Ministry.

Adult Bible Studies

Adults at Shenandoah Baptist Church gather throughout the week for Bible study, prayer, and discussion.

These gatherings offer opportunities to grow spiritually while forming genuine relationships with others in the church.

Learn more about Adult Bible Studies.

Youth / Teen Bible Studies

Youth and Teenagers at Shenandoah Baptist Church gather once a week for Bible study on Wednesday evenings starting at 6pm for prayer, snacks / dinner and discussion.

Youth are welcome to join for music, games, and God.

1 Timothy 4:12

Learn more about Youth Bible Studies.

Women's Ministry

Women meet regularly for Bible study and fellowship, creating space for encouragement, spiritual growth, and friendship across generations.

Learn more about Women's Ministry.

Men's Fellowship

Men gather for occasional lunches and fellowship throughout the year. These gatherings provide a relaxed opportunity to connect, encourage one another, and build community.

Learn more about Men's Fellowship.

Music Ministry

Our music ministry welcomes anyone who wants to sing, play an instrument, or help support worship through audio and visual technology. Whether you are experienced or simply interested in learning, there is a place for you to participate. There is no cost to join—just a willingness to give a little time and a heart for worship. You’re welcome to attend a rehearsal, observe, or try participating to see if it’s a good fit. Opportunities include our worship choir, instrumentalists, and audio visual team.

Learn more about the Music Ministry.

Fellowship & Special Events

Throughout the year, our church hosts fellowship meals, holiday gatherings, and community events that bring people together.

These events are a wonderful way to build friendships and celebrate life as a church family.

View upcoming events.

Find Your Place

At Shenandoah Baptist Church, ministry is about more than programs — it's about people. We believe everyone has a place to grow, serve, and belong.

If you're looking for a welcoming church community in Cedar Park, we invite you to explore these ministries and join us.
