Men's Ministry

Men's Ministry in Cedar Park

At Shenandoah Baptist Church, our men's ministry focuses on building strong Christian friendships, encouraging spiritual growth, and supporting men as they lead their families in faith.

In a small Baptist church setting, men have the opportunity to truly know one another, pray together, and walk through life's challenges and victories side by side.

Whether you are new to church, new to the area, or simply looking for a place where your family can grow in faith, our men's gatherings provide a welcoming place to connect with other Christian men.

Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of our neighbors and the nations through evangelism, baptism, and biblical teaching.

Weekly Gathering

Tuesday Morning Prayer

6:30 AM

Each Tuesday morning, men gather at the church for a dedicated time of prayer before the day begins.

During this time, men pray for their families, the church, the community, and one another.

Tuesday morning prayer is a meaningful way to start the day centered on faith. The group shares prayer requests and encourages one another spiritually as they begin the week.

Men's Fellowship

Men's Fellowship Lunch

From time to time, the men of the church gather for fellowship lunches. These relaxed gatherings are a great opportunity to share a meal, build friendships, and connect with other men in the church community.

Conversations often include faith, family life, work, and the everyday experiences of being a Christian man.

These meals help strengthen relationships and create a sense of brotherhood within the church.

Why Men Appreciate Our Ministry

  • A welcoming environment for men of all ages
  • Regular opportunities for prayer and encouragement
  • Meaningful friendships and fellowship
  • Support for men as husbands, fathers, and leaders

Join Us

While our men's ministry currently centers around prayer and fellowship, we hope to continue growing additional opportunities in the future as more men become involved.

If you are looking for a small Baptist church where men can grow spiritually and build genuine friendships, we invite you to join us.

There is always room at the table and a place for you here.
