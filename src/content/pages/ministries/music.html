Music Ministry

Church Music Ministry in Cedar Park

The music ministry at Shenandoah Baptist Church is open to anyone who wants to worship, learn, and participate. Whether you have years of musical experience or simply enjoy singing, there is a place for you here.

Joining the music ministry doesn't cost anything—just a willingness to give a little time and a heart for worship.

You are always welcome to come watch a rehearsal, try it out, or participate to see if it's a good fit. There is no long-term commitment required to start.

Our goal is simple: to help lead our church family in worship and encourage one another through music.

Worship Choir

Sunday Worship Choir

Our worship choir helps lead music during Sunday morning services and welcomes anyone middle school age or older who enjoys singing.

You do not need formal training or special experience—just a desire to sing and worship together.

The easiest way to join is simply to come to Wednesday night rehearsal and introduce yourself to the worship leader.

New visitors and brand-new church members are always welcome to participate.

Musicians & Special Music

Instrumentalists

Musicians who play instruments and would like to participate in the worship ministry are encouraged to speak with the worship leader.

Depending on the instrument and role, a short audition may be requested so we can find the best way for you to serve within the worship team.

Our goal is not performance—it's helping lead the congregation in worship in a meaningful way.

Special Music

If you would like to sing a special song during a service, please contact the worship leader to arrange a time to review the music together.

Audio Visual Ministry

The audio visual team supports the worship service by managing sound and visual elements during services and events.

This team helps ensure that music, speaking, and presentations run smoothly.

If you would like to be part of the audio visual ministry, please contact Brett Brewer.

Experience with audio or video equipment is helpful but not required. Training can be provided. Because this team supports live services, members are asked to be dependable and committed to their scheduled times.

Why People Enjoy Serving in Music Ministry

  • Opportunities to use musical gifts to serve the church
  • A welcoming environment for singers and musicians
  • Encouragement through worship and fellowship
  • Training and opportunities to grow in ministry

Join the Music Ministry

The music ministry at Shenandoah Baptist Church is about more than singing or playing instruments. It's about using the gifts God has given us to serve the church and encourage others in worship.

Whether you want to sing in the choir, play an instrument, help with audio and visuals, or simply come observe a rehearsal, you are welcome.

If you're looking for a church music ministry in Cedar Park where you can participate and grow, we invite you to join us.
