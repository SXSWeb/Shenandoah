Adult Bible Study

Bible Study in Cedar Park

Studying the Bible together is an important part of life at Shenandoah Baptist Church. Our adult Bible studies provide a welcoming place to explore Scripture, ask questions, and grow in faith alongside others in the community.

If you are looking for a Bible study in Cedar Park, we invite you to join us. Whether you are new to church or have studied the Bible for years, our classes are open to everyone.

Sunday Morning Bible Study

Coffee & Donuts

9:00 AM – 9:30 AM

Sunday mornings begin with a relaxed time of coffee and donuts in the lobby, giving people a chance to connect and meet one another before class begins.

Adult Bible Study Class

9:30 AM – 10:30 AM

Led by Pastor Marcos

The Sunday morning Bible study focuses on studying Scripture together with thoughtful discussion and practical application for everyday life.

Participants are encouraged to listen, ask questions, and contribute to the conversation.

Wednesday Evening Bible Study

Midweek Bible Study

Wednesdays • 6:30 PM – 7:30 PM

Led by Wes Griffen

Our midweek Bible study offers another opportunity to pause during the week and spend time in God’s Word.

This gathering includes Bible teaching, discussion, and encouragement as we grow together in faith.

Many families attend while their children and teenagers participate in other Wednesday night ministries at the church.

Why People Enjoy Our Bible Studies

  • Welcoming and discussion-based classes
  • Practical teaching from Scripture
  • Opportunities to ask questions and grow in faith
  • Friendships formed through shared study and conversation

Join a Bible Study

Adult Bible studies at Shenandoah Baptist Church are welcoming and discussion-oriented. Whether you are exploring faith, returning to church after time away, or simply wanting to grow deeper in your understanding of the Bible, you will find a friendly group ready to learn together.

If you’re searching for a Christian Bible study in Cedar Park, we would love for you to join us this week.
