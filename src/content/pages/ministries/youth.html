Youth Ministry

Youth Group in Cedar Park

The youth ministry at Shenandoah Baptist Church provides a welcoming place for students to build friendships, grow in faith, and talk honestly about life and God.

If you are looking for a youth group in Cedar Park for middle school or high school students, we invite your teen to join us.

Our youth gatherings are relaxed, welcoming, and focused on helping students develop a meaningful relationship with God while connecting with others their age.

Weekly Youth Group

Wednesday Nights

6:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Youth group is open to students in 6th through 12th grade.

Wednesday evenings are a time for students to gather together in a friendly and encouraging environment.

The evening typically includes:

  • Games and activities
  • A shared meal or snacks
  • Prayer and encouragement
  • Conversations about faith and life

Students often arrive hungry after school and activities, so meals or snacks are always appreciated when families are able to contribute.

A Place to Belong

Our youth group is intentionally welcoming and smaller in size, which allows students to build genuine friendships and feel comfortable participating.

Teens are encouraged to ask questions, share their thoughts, and grow in their understanding of faith in a supportive environment.

Additional Opportunities

Music & Worship

After youth group, students are also welcome to stay and observe or participate in choir rehearsal if they are interested in music and worship.

Join Us Wednesday Night

Whether your student has grown up in church or is simply curious about faith, they are welcome to join us.

If you are searching for a teen youth group in Cedar Park, we would love to meet your family and welcome your student to youth night.
