Women’s Ministry

Women’s Ministry in Cedar Park

At Shenandoah Baptist Church, our women’s ministry creates space for friendship, encouragement, creativity, and spiritual growth. Women gather throughout the week to study the Bible, share life together, and support one another in faith.

If you’re looking for a women’s Bible study or women’s group in Cedar Park, we would love to welcome you.

Weekly Bible Study

Thursday Morning Study

Led by: Zora

10:30 AM – 12:30 PM

Our weekly Bible studies are led by Zora and provide a welcoming environment where women can grow in their understanding of Scripture while building meaningful friendships.

Thursday Evening Study

Led by: Fran Crowder

7:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Women of all ages and backgrounds are welcome to join us for Bible teaching, discussion, and encouragement as we learn together and support one another in faith.

Sew Many Ways – Sewing & Craft Group

Creative Sewing & Craft Time

For women who enjoy sewing, quilting, or crafting, our Sew Many Ways group offers a relaxed and creative space to learn new skills and share projects with others.

This group meets at the church on Tuesdays (except holidays) and welcomes women with all levels of experience—from beginners to experienced sewers.

Participants often work on projects such as:

  • Quilts
  • Clothing
  • Embroidery
  • Knitting
  • Other creative crafts

Those working on quilts typically piece them by machine and complete quilting by machine or by hand. Patterns and some equipment may be available to borrow.

You do not need to attend the church to participate. Everyone is welcome.

Contact: Fran Crowder for more information.

Why Women Enjoy Our Ministry

  • Welcoming environment for women of all ages
  • Opportunities to grow in faith through Bible study
  • Creative outlets through sewing and crafts
  • Meaningful friendships and encouragement

Join Us

Whether you join a Bible study or the sewing group, our women’s ministry is about building real friendships while growing in faith and community.

Women from Cedar Park and surrounding communities are always welcome to join us.
